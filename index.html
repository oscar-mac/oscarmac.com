I am currently a full-stack developer at Linked by Air, a design and technology studio based in Brooklyn, New York. My formative background is in graphic design, and I hold a B.F.A. in Communication Design from Parsons School of Design. Before university, I studied music at the Booker T. Washington High School for the Performing and Visual Arts in Dallas, Texas. In my free time, I like to cook.

I work on a wide range of projects at Linked by Air. These are some of the projects I have contributed to the most:

  • Yale School of Architecture Advanced Technology
  • Data for Black Lives
  • Out of Many: National Academy of Design
  • Deborah Berke Partners
  • Columbia GSAPP
